TITLE: U.S. DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE
SERVINGS: 2
COST: $$$$

INGREDIENTS:
1 cup cooked brown rice
1/2 cup fat free (skim) milk, or 1%
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
https://www.myplate.gov/recipes/rice-bowl-breakfast-fruit-and-nuts 1/5

DIRECTIONS:
Wash hands with soap and water.
Combine cooked rice, milk and cinnamon in a microwave-safe bowl. Microwave on HIGH for 45 seconds. Stir and heat for another 45 to 60 seconds, or until rice mixture is heated through.
Divide rice mixture between two bowls. Top with fruit and nuts. Serve warm.
Refrigerate leftovers within 2 hours. Notes Use any combination of fresh, canned, frozen, and dried fruit in this recipe. You can use any leftover cooked grain in this recipe. Try white or wild rice, quinoa, barley, or oatmeal. Skim milk, apples, and walnuts were used for nutrition and cost analysis. Learn more about fruits and what's in season now.
