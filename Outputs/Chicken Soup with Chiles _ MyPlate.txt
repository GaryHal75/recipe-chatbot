Chicken Soup with Chiles
Makes: 6 servings
Total Cost: $$$$
Flavorful and filling, this soup is packed with protein making it a full meal. Add extra chiles or a
dash of red pepper flakes if you like a spicier soup.
Ingredients
3 pounds chicken pieces, skin removed
2 cups tomatoes, chopped
1 garlic clove, minced
1/2 cup onion, chopped
1/4 cup mild chiles, canned and diced
2 cups pinto or garbanzo beans, canned or cooked and drained
salt and pepper (to taste, optional)
Directions
1. Wash hands with soap and water.
2. Place chicken pieces in a large saucepan and add enough water to cover.
